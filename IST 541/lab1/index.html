How to Complete the Direct Instruction Training

By Emily Cummins


Step 1: Select "Pretest"

Get ready to begin your Direct Instruction Training. Once you have navigated to the training website you need to start by completing a pretest to see what you already know.Go ahead and click on the first module button "Pretest".


Step 2: Complete the Pretest

Participate in the pretest and be sure to type your school email in the box so that you can recieve your results. Don't forget to click submit to ensure that your results are saved and sent to you.



Step 3: Complete Modules 1-6

Now it is time to begin the modules. Complete the six modules by clicking on each Module Button. When you have finished a module it will send you back to the homepage and from there you can move on to the next.


Step 4: Select "Posttest"

You have now completed the training and need to participate in the posttest.The posttest is the same as the pretest. This is how we will see how much you have learned!


Step 5: Complete the Posttest

Repeat Step 2. Make sure you type in your school email address and submit your response in order it to be recorded.


Step 6: Select "Survey"

This is your last step in the training! We would love your feedback about the training. Please give your honest responses in the survey.


Step 7: Complete the survey

Repeat Step 2 and Step 5. Make sure you type in your email and submit the survey so that your responses can be recorded.
